### 🫡 the actual story

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student who got curious about how things break — and accidentally fell into Cyber Security, Networking, and AI because of it. Most of what I know didn't come from a textbook; it came from staring at an error message for way too long until it finally made sense.

Right now I'm wrestling with **Pattern Recognition** and **Neural Networks** for coursework, and trying to convince myself that the math will eventually click. Linux is my home base — not because I'm a hacker, just because GUI settings menus genuinely confuse me more than the terminal does.

I like building things that solve a real (or at least real-ish) problem, even if the "solution" is held together by duct tape and three Stack Overflow tabs.
